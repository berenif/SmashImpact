✅ Enemy Movement Fix Applied

The enemy rushing behavior has been fixed!

Changes Made:

  • Added ATTACK_RANGE (50 units) - enemies stop approaching when within this distance
  • Added RETREAT_RANGE (30 units) - enemies back away if too close to the player
  • Implemented circling behavior - enemies orbit around the player when in attack range
  • Enemies alternate between clockwise and counter-clockwise circling based on their ID

Expected Behavior:

  • ✅ Enemies will approach the player until they reach 50 units distance
  • ✅ Enemies will circle around the player when in attack range (30-50 units)
  • ✅ Enemies will back away if they get closer than 30 units
  • ✅ No more rushing directly at the player
